Our Mission

Bhakti365 is your daily spiritual companion, bringing ancient wisdom and devotion into your modern life. We provide daily Panchanga, festival information, sacred shlokas, and spiritual guidance to help you stay connected with your spiritual roots in a serene, ad-free environment.

Core Features

Designed carefully with custom utilities to make daily spiritual practice seamless and mindful:

  • Daily Panchanga with accurate local astronomical calculations.
  • Comprehensive festival calendar tracking solar, lunar, and regional calendar events.
  • Sacred shlokas and mantras with high-quality recorded loops and meaning guides.
  • Interactive Jap Mala clicker with rounds completed tracking.
  • Rashi calculator for moon sign and birth star insights.
  • Nearby temple finder for discovering historical and prominent deities nearby.
